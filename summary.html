Here are a few tips on spotting GenAI content:

  • Paragraph and capitalizing structure: AI tends to create numerous paragraphs, often breaking content into shorter chunks than a human writer might, with capitalized headlines that actually make the final result harder to read.
  • Lists overuse: Frequent bulleted or numbered lists throughout the content
  • Formulaic conclusions: It is quite common to see AI-generated content concluding with a formulaic statement like "In conclusion..." or "To sum up..."
  • Factual inaccuracies: Watch for "hallucinations" or factual errors that a knowledgeable human wouldn't make
  • Heavy ad monetization: Many AI-generated pages feature excessive advertising

Remember that experienced AI operators can instruct their systems to avoid these telltale signs, requiring an innovative approach to detect GenAI at scale.
